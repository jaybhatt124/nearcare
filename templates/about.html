About NearCares

Empowering patients to find the right care at the right time

🏥 NearCares

Our Mission

NearCares is an AI-powered platform built to bridge the gap between patients and the right healthcare specialists. We believe finding the right doctor should be simple, fast, and free.

Using your live GPS location, we instantly surface hospitals and clinics grouped by specialty — so whether you have heart pain or a skin issue, you see exactly which type of facility to visit.

📍
Live Location Search

High-accuracy GPS positioning with fallback to manual address entry

🏥
Specialty Grouping

Hospitals automatically sorted by 15+ medical specialties

🗄️
Verified Hospital Database

Admin-curated hospital list with accurate location and specialty data

🔍
Disease Search

Search any condition — built-in or custom — to find matching specialists

Our Vision

A future where no patient is lost searching for the right specialist — healthcare navigation that is instant, intelligent, and accessible to everyone.

🌍

Universal Access

Make specialist healthcare findable for every person, regardless of location, language, or literacy — across rural towns and metro cities alike.

⚡

Zero-Wait Discovery

Eliminate the confusion of "which hospital for what condition." GPS + smart specialty matching means the right answer in under 5 seconds.

🤝

Patient-First Always

No ads, no paid rankings, no bias. Every result is ranked purely by proximity and specialization — because your health is not a business opportunity.

🔬

Smarter Over Time

Continuously improving hospital data, expanding disease coverage, and integrating AI to better understand symptoms and connect patients with the right care.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

Your location and health data are never stored or sold. Every search is ephemeral — we exist to help you, not to profile you.

🏗️

Built for India

Designed with India's healthcare landscape in mind — multi-city hospital coverage, regional specialty gaps, and the reality of navigating a complex public-private system.
